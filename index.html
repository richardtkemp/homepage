About

I spent years in financial services building data systems, ETL pipelines, and operational infrastructure. I left to do something more useful: help small and mid-sized businesses navigate the noise around AI and automation, and find the things that actually save them time and money.

I lead with questions, not pitches. Most of the value I deliver starts with understanding what's actually slowing a business down—before anyone writes a line of code or signs up for a platform. Sometimes the answer is a £50/month tool. Sometimes it's a custom build. Sometimes it's "don't automate this at all."

What I Offer

  • Discovery calls—free, 20–30 minutes. I map your workflows and identify bottlenecks. No pitch, no obligation. You'll leave with ideas you can act on even if we never work together.
  • AI readiness assessments—a focused engagement to map your processes, rank the automation opportunities by ROI, and deliver a prioritised roadmap. You can take it to anyone.
  • Implementation—I design it, build it, and hand it over working. No outsourcing, no "I'll coordinate with our technical team." Start small, prove value, then scale.

I'm vendor-neutral. I don't sell platforms or tools. If an off-the-shelf solution solves your problem, I'll tell you. My background in finance means I think in ROI, payback periods, and cost/benefit—not vague "productivity gains."

It's the infrastructure I use daily—my own AI copilot runs on it.

Coordination between people, mediated by their agents.
